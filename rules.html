CPPS.to Rules

This is a list of the CPPS.to rules. We don't have many, but the ones we do have are intended to keep you and other players safe! Please do your best to obey them or you will be punished accordingly.

No sharing personal information

We do not allow sharing of personal information, of yourself or others. This is intended to keep you safe!


No abusing bugs or glitches

We do not allow the abuse and exploitation of bugs or any means of reverse engineering CPPS.to for malicious purposes.


No Spam or Sexual Content

Spam and Sexual content is not allowed in public rooms. Spam includes noise, emote, and chat messages. This rule is not applied in igloos.


Absolutely no advertising

Advertising any other CPPS or malicious/adult related websites is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.


Harassment, racism, bullying

We do not allow the harassment or bullying of other players. Please refrain from taking part of this kind of activity or your account could be banned. Can't we all just get along?
