Clothing/Player Commands:

  • !REALNICK - gives you your real username back after a room change.
  • !AI *ID* - Replace *ID* with the ID of the item you want to add.
  • !AF *ID* - Replace *ID* with the ID of the furniture you want to add.
  • !AS *ID* - Replace *ID* with the ID of the stamp you want to add.
  • !AC 5000 - Adds 5000 coins to your user.
  • !PIN *ID* - Allows you to use any item as pin, replace *ID* with the item's ID.
  • !UP NORMAL - Gives you your proper username/nickname back; but removes all of your clothing. - Use !REALNICK to get your real nickname back without losing your outfit. Change rooms after use.
  • !UP *INITIALS* - Put the initials of a 'mascot' - eg: RH (Rockhopper), AA (Aunt Arctic), etc, or try combinations such as !UP Rockhopper, as some of them are coded for ease of use!
  • !UP BILLYBOB/SCREENHOG/HAPPY77/GIZMO/RSNAIL - gives you their outfit & name.
  • !SANTA - Become Santa ;0
  • !ROOKIE - Makes you rookie.
  • !CAR - become a car ;-)
  • !NG - Instantly refresh your nameglow in game

Serverstats commands:

  • !PING - This command was made to check if you're still connected to the server.
  • !PONG - This command was made to check if you're still connected to the server.
  • !UPTIME - Shows the server's uptime.
  • !RAM - Shows the server's RAM usage.

Igloo commands:

  • !IGLOO *ID* - Replace *ID* with the ID of the igloo you want to have.
  • !FLOOR *ID* - Updates the floor to the given ID.
  • !MUSIC *ID* - Updates the music to the given ID.
  • !UI *ID* - Same function as !IGLOO.
  • !UF *ID* - Same function as !FLOOR.
  • !UM *ID* - Same function as !MUSIC.

Other commands:

  • !JR *ID* - Beams you to the room ID you defined.
